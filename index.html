Thumbnails created in Adobe Illustrator & Webpage created in HTML & CSS by Brittany Batchelder 03/14/2022

Hello, there! This is my very first Webpage adorned with my very own illustrated thumbnails, entirely created from scratch with CSS & HTML code! Up until this point, I've mainly been an Illustrator who worked with publishing firms, pitching my personal projects, specifically Tarot Decks. I graduated in 2019 with my Bachelors in Fine Arts. Since then, I've also worked with clients in providing one-on-one commission pieces. Recently, I've been working towards building my Graphic Design portfolio and learned that one has to become familiar with front end coding. This webpage is the result of teaching myself the basics of coding in the span of three weeks.


Go ahead and click on any of the following 48 icons, they will take you to websites relavant to myself as an individual. Spanning from my upbrining in New Hampshire, to my ancestry spanning all the way back to the puritains. You will also learn a little bit about the history of New England and the State of Ohio, alongside the origins of my first and last name. This was a lot of fun to do, and super stoked to add this to my Graphic Design portfolio!

  • For a more immersive experience, click on the thumbnails from top to bottom, left to right.


  • The more immersive experience will seamlessly flow themes, context, and content from one subject matter to the next in a more digestable format.


  • Enjoy interacting with my very first webpage! I look forward to building more webpages in the near future!
